Understanding the Visualization

Team Type Colors: Teams are color-coded based on whether they were "trendy" (blue), "trend-defying" (orange), or neither (gray) for each season.

Available Teams: You can search for any NBA team using their abbreviation (e.g., HOU, GSW, BOS, LAL, MIA, etc.)

Metrics Explained:

  • Win Percentage: Team's winning percentage for the season
  • Average Height: Average height of the team's roster in inches
  • Height Standard Deviation: Standard deviation of heights (measure of height diversity)

The charts show how the team has evolved over time in terms of roster construction (height metrics) and performance (win percentage).
